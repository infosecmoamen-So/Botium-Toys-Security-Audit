# Botium Toys: Regulatory Compliance Checklist

This document analyzes international regulations and compliance parameters governing Botium Toys' business operations due to its expanding global footprint and digital transaction processing[cite: 3, 4].

## 📋 Regulatory Assessment

### ☒ General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR)
* **Compliance Status:** **Mandatory Requirement**
* **Analysis & Justification:** Because Botium Toys operates an online e-commerce market that attracts international customers and actively processes the personal data of European Union (E.U.) citizens, GDPR compliance is legally required[cite: 3, 4]. Botium Toys must uphold strict data privacy rights, apply robust administrative protections to consumer logs, and commit to notifying impacted individuals within 72 hours in the event of an infrastructure breach[cite: 4].

### ☒ Payment Card Industry Data Security Standard (PCI DSS)
* **Compliance Status:** **Mandatory Requirement**[cite: 4]
* **Analysis & Justification:** Botium Toys processes online credit and debit card payments worldwide via its expanding e-commerce platform[cite: 3, 4]. To avoid severe financial penalties and fraud liabilities, the company is mandated to store, transmit, and handle all credit cardholder metrics within an encrypted, technically hardened environment that satisfies PCI DSS standards[cite: 4, 5].

### ☒ System and Organization Controls (SOC 1 / SOC 2)
* **Compliance Status:** **Mandatory Requirement**[cite: 4]
* **Analysis & Justification:** As the IT department scales to support global distribution networks, executing SOC audits is critical to verify user access management policies[cite: 3, 4]. SOC frameworks evaluate Botium Toys' controls concerning data confidentiality, security infrastructure, integrity, and operational availability to minimize corporate risk and avoid internal operational fraud[cite: 4].

---

## 🚫 Non-Applicable Standards

### ☐ Federal Energy Regulatory Commission (FERC-NERC)
* **Compliance Status:** **Not Applicable**[cite: 4]
* **Analysis & Justification:** FERC-NERC regulations apply uniquely to organizations managing Bulk Power Systems and physical electric grid infrastructure across North America[cite: 4]. Since Botium Toys specializes exclusively in manufacturing and selling commercial toys, it holds no exposure or legal obligation to critical energy infrastructure mandates[cite: 3, 4].

### ☐ Health Insurance Portability and Accountability Act (HIPAA)
* **Compliance Status:** **Not Applicable**[cite: 4]
* **Analysis & Justification:** HIPAA is a strict U.S. federal framework designed to govern Protected Health Information (PHI) held by healthcare providers and medical insurance entities[cite: 4]. Botium Toys handles retail consumer transaction records and has no involvement with medical history or clinical data collection[cite: 3, 4].
